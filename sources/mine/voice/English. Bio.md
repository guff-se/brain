Gustaf is one of Sweden’s most frequently hired speakers on the topics of digital transformation, trends and technology. He speaks about the societal impacts of the rapid change driven by technological evolution.

He has an academic background in cognitive science, rhetoric, chemistry, law, biomedical engineering and business. His ventures range from a nation wide entrepreneurship competition to digital tool for political data analysis. In 2014, he started Sweden’s first 3D Print Shop, a makerspace, startup incubator and technology education platform. Over the last 13 years, he's created up Europe's largest co-created culture festival with over 4000 participants from 25 countries. Along with his wife he's created art displayed at Stockholm’s National Museum of Art, Zona Maco and ArtMiami.

As a speaker, Gustaf leads the audience through complex subjects by using a creative mix of science, storytelling and humor. He has a way of connecting new trinkets and trends with deep underlying themes of humanity and our future.

Gustaf gives talks about creativity, business, and entrepreneurial leadership. He is the founder and CEO of The Entrepreneurial Chase (Entreprenörsjakten), a business consultancy that brings the mindset of young entrepreneurs into established corporations. After a successful run in 2009, the company has now spread to 4 cities and involves 120 young entrepreneurs in the spring of 2010. A lover of participatory art, Gustaf organized live roleplaying games in his youth, worked with the collaborative art project Futuredrome and is set on bringing swedish culture to Burning Man in the Nevada dessert. Gustaf is also the chairman of Young Entrepreneurs of Sweden, a nation wide network of entrepreneurs under 30.

Gustaf is the founder of the Entrepreneurial Chase, a business-focused scavenger hunt where young entrepreneurs help companies solve real problems. He also gives talks on creativity and entrepreneurial leadership, drawing lessons from both academic research and real-world experience.

Starting his first company at 19, Gustaf has started projects in everything from events, theater and music to web development, marketing and politics. He studied Cognitive Science at University of Gothenburg, Rhetoric, Chemistry and Law at Stockholm University, and Biomedical Engineering and Business at Illinois Institute of Technology where he graduated cum laude with a B.S. in Psychology.

He’s the founder of the Entrepreneurial Chase, a competition that has engaged over 200 young entrepreneurs over three years, and was elected the first president of Young Entrepreneurs of Sweden in 2009. Other initiatives include Riksdagsrösten and Idépedia. Besides his own projects he’s done consulting work for everything from cafés to web startups.

Gustaf has been a guest lecturer at Stockholm School of Entrepreneurship and Hyper Island’s Master Classes, as well as done inspirational talks for thousands of high school and college students.
