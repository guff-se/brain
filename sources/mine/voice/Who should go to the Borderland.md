**Who should go to the Borderland? Help shape our community!**

We’re a small, but fast growing community. As with any "chaotic system", initial conditions will affect the outcome in very radical ways. Who comes to The Borderland 2014 and what takes place there will have a big influence of the direction that our dreams and our future will take. That’s why I would like you all to help us make the decision of who should come.

In this age of social media, it is very easy to “market” an event like this, to call out to everyone “CHECK THIS SHIT OUT!!!”. If you strongly feel you want to do that, please do. But I would like to suggest a more personal approach, an *intentional* approach.

I would personally like ask you all to make a list of your “top ten”. The ten people in the world that you most of all would like at The Borderland. These could be friends and lovers, or people that inspire you, that you’ve never even met. Either way, create your list and make them come.

Call them. Buy them lunch or dinner. Tell them about The Borderland. Tell them about your dreams. Hold their hand and help them take the step towards their own.

Who you decide to invite is a form of self-expression, don't simply surrender it to the viral structures of Facebook. This is an opportunity for **you** to shape our world.

Much love,

/ Gustaf
