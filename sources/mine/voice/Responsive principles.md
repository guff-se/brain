**You are responsible for You**  
You are encouraged to discover, exercise and rely on your inner resources. You are entitled to nothing, invited to everything. If at any time during our time together you find yourself in any situation where you are neither learning nor contributing, **use your two feet**, go someplace else. Do not ask for permission.  
  
**You are here to Give**  
You are invited because you **have something to give**. Only you, yourself will know exactly what that is that you give best. Gifts are **unconditional**, they do not contemplate a return for something of equal value.  
  
**You are here to Play**  
We believe that learning has to be intrinsically motivated, and that true **curiosity** is deeply connected with a sense of play. As you arrive here, we invite you to be completely engaged with what you are doing in the **here** and **now**. And instead of calling it work, realize it is **play**.  
  
**You are here to Express**  
We want to hear your thoughts, beliefs, ideas, and contributions with encouragment and no judgments. We invite **vulnerability**. All that is you is welcome here.  
  
**You are here to Participate**  
This is not a gathering for passive spectators. Your collaboration, co-creation, and participation is what makes this experience possible. Enter with energy, say **“yes, and”.** Be mindful of the process.
