Ten years has passed since the jolly hackers of the Pirate Bay first set out to sea.

Ten years of dauntless pursuit of freedom and curiosity in the world of the electron and the switch. With our retractable batons drawn, flags waiving and servers trimmed we have boldly sailed against the winds of the gods and the international copyright mob.

We have fought and won. We have fought and lost. But, we are many and for ten years we have prevailed!

It is time for celebration!

Bring on the fear, the loathing and the jolly debauchery in honor of our infamous Pirates of Freedom!

This is our finest hour!
