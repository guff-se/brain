Participatory culture, co-creation, Burning Man and the evolution of consciousness. Startups, experiments, mvp’s, innovation and disruption. Self-management, metamodernism, teal, integral. Curiosity, empathy, humility and self-esteem. These are a few of my favorite things. (No, the melody doesn’t match, don’t even try it).

Hello, lovely people. Thank you for inviting me to this prestigious group of highly functional individuals. I thought I’d introduce myself in case we run in to each other at some cool Hyper Project of the Future.

I’ve been speaking at a couple of master classes back in 2010-2011. Since 2013, I’ve been the industry leader for the Exploring Technology module for DMC in Stockholm, getting 80 students to build a co-created interactive art exhibition. 2013-2016 I founded and ran Stockholms first commercial makerspace, Makerspark, a 200sqm store, workshop, co-working and event space in old town. I’m currently involved in the toy company Strawbees, and Sweden’s largest Bitcoin market, [bt.cx](http://bt.cx).

Burning Man has been my second home since 2009, and worked some with the Burning Man organization both in US and internationally. I’ve been the main organizer for The Borderland, a co-created, self-managed festival that has now grown to 1800 people outside of Copenhagen. I also like helping my wife Malin build awesome interactive optical fibre textile art installations ([www.malinbobeck.se](http://www.malinbobeck.se)).

I grew up on stage and have a talent for speaking (both about things I know, and things I don’t know). I’ve done about 70 speaking gigs in the last year, and I’m nominated for speaker of the year in Sweden. Currently, 90% of my speaking is in Sweden, and I would love to get out more. This is one of the reasons I’m reconnecting with this group. Let’s go play!
