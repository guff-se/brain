The light you see flowing through the textile.  That’s a recording of Malin’s breath.  
We used a face mask to track the flow of her breathing as she was meditating.  That data drives a generative algorithm makes the artwork come alive.  
Every breath is mathematically generated to be unique.  Every breath is only shown once.
