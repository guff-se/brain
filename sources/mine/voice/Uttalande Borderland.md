There has been an accident at Boesdal Kalkbrud during the festival The Borderland.

One of our participants was found in the water at 04.18 am on Friday morning. CPR was started immediately and continued until the ambulance crew took over. At 09.30, he passed away at Copenhagen Rikshospital. A structure has been put in place to support the community members affected by the incident. His family has been informed and our thoughts go out to them and all his friends.

If you have questions, please contact [info@theborderland.se](mailto:info@theborderland.se) or +4542152791.
