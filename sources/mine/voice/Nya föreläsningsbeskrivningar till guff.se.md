1. The Meaning Economy

What happens in a world where both consumers and employees grown up never knowing anything but the top of Maslow's pyramid? In a world were monetary incentives become less effective, employers and brands have to deliver something more. They have to provides people's life with meaning.

This session will tie together religious philosophy and the human chase for meaning, with the rise of the internet, social media and the maker movement and give new perspectives on what drives the affluent citizen of the 21st century.

2. The Maker Movement

3D Printing, Open Hardware, Hackerspaces and Crowdfunding. New technology and new practices are leveling the playing field for innovators and entrepreneurs. The development of new products and services are becoming faster and cheaper than ever.

I'm the founder of Makerspark, the first commercial Makerspace in Sweden and the largest consumer 3D print shop in Europe. I'd like to share some of my thoughts about technological and social change, and talk about how the maker movement empowers people and disrupts businesses.

3. Business Model Workshop

The Business Model Canvas is a visual representation of how your business work. In this workshop you get to understaantnd how to use the canvas as a tool for understanding and developing new business and marketing ideas. Depending on context, this could also include a session on Lean Startup, to give an understanding on how to use the ideas generated by the canvas to create and run experiments.

This workshop could for example be used in combination with the "Mashup" workshop, that Mikael Ahlström does (and that I did in the recent HIMCs in Amsterdam and GBG), to make the Mashup workshop more relevant. You do this by first mapping out resources inside the client company and then use those as a part of the mashup workshop to create new products and services. Would probably work great as a part of a tailored master class. 

2. "The Maker Movement"

3D Printing, Open Hardware, Hackerspaces och Crowdfunding. Ny teknologi och nya metoder har jämnat ut startfältet för innovatörer och entreprenörer. Utvecklingen av nya produkter och tjänster blir allt snabbare och allt billigare. Vi är mitt i en boom av kreativ mångfald!

Gustaf är grundare till Makerspark, Sveriges första “3D print shop”, en kreativ verkstad för ny teknik. I denna föreläsning delar han några av sina tankar om ny teknologi och den samhällsförändring som den tekniska utveckligen för med sig. En beskrivning av “The Maker Movement”, en rörelse som ger makten åt folket och ställer traditionella företag på sin ända.
