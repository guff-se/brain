Hi,
My name is Gustaf and I am an entrepreneur.

I've studied Cognitive Science at Gothenburg University, moved to Stockholm to study Rhetoric, switched to Chemistry and did Law on the side. I moved to Chicago to take up Biomedical Engineering, switched to Business and got a degree in Psychology. I call it en entrepreneurial education.

For me, it's aways been about coming dreaming up new ideas and finding ways to make them real. It's not about money, fame or prestige. I just get a kick out of finding out if ideas I come up with actually work.

First, come up with an idea.

Second, I make it happen.

Then ask yourself: did it work?

If it didn't, come up with a new idea.

However, if it did work, you get someone else to do it for you.

And then start again.
