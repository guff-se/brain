when those wings are just not flying me fast enough

when thoughts rush and mind travels further than any light or matter

when I reach for what’s next, to find it present, past, passé

when the mental prototypes of my fleeting ideas are created, already dated

that’s when i bite my nails, and as i keep pushing forward

i once again find myself

back where i started.
