Panelist at The Creators Conference
Tomorrow I will be appearing in a panel at the Creators Conference at Hotel Rival, Stockholm. Since I first got the question to be on the panel, I've been asking myself why I really belong there. Yes, sure, I label myself creator, but I wasn't aware that other people bought it. A creator is supposed to be working in a "creative field", such as music, theatre or art.
