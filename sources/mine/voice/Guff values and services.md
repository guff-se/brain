**Our Values:**

- We love to fail.

- We don't know anything.

- We don't cost anything.

- …and we have fun doing it!

**Our Services:**

Innovation Services

We help you innovate. Through organizational development, process design, education individual coaching and workshops, we help you to foster innovation in all parts of your company.

StartUp on Demand

A Startup is a human institution designed to create new products and services under conditions of extreme uncertainty* We create and host those institutions for you, bringing together ideas, talent and process to accelerate your innovation process.

Entrepreneurs in Residence

In our EiR-program, a successful entrepreneur will work inside your business for a couple of hours a week or days a month over an extended time, bringing his or her experience of the fast paced entrepreneurial playing field in to your organization.

**StartUp on Demand:**

For corporations:

Maybe you don't have the structures in place to take care of and examine new innovations? Our StartUp on Demand provides a fast and cheap way of prototyping innovations and business ideas. We put together a team, host the process and help you discover the potential (or problems) with your idea.

For angels:

Are you investing in a green entrepreneur that needs structure and focus? We can help you host the startup and provide process and metrics to accelerate learning and make sure that entrepreneurial success (or failure) comes faster.
