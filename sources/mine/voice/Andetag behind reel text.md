The word "Andetag" is Swedish, and means "Breath." It's a combination of two words, "ande", meaning "spirit", and "tag", meaning "to take." I think it's a beautiful analogy, to take in the spirit with every new breath.  
Just by becoming aware of our breath and adjusting the way we use it, we can make changes to our nervous system and our bodies. Your breath is a powerful tool for drawing your mind into the present moment.   
  
In this series, i've been working with a vertical symmetry, the symmetry of the human body, the symetry of life. The animation allows the viewer to synchronize their breath with the artwork, creating an harmonious connection. Every piece is synchronized via the internet with every other, creating a global rhythm and gathering the world in one collective breath.
