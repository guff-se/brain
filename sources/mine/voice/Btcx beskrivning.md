Gustaf Josefsson is an entrepreneur, futurologist and one of Sweden's most frequently hired speakers on the topic of emergent technology.

"To me, Bitcoin is one of the most politically disruptive and philosophically interesting technologies brought on by the internet. It is not just challenging how we exchange currency; it is questioning what authority is and how power is created. Having used Bitcoin since 2010, I am excited to get to work with btcx and watch more closely as this industry evolves."
