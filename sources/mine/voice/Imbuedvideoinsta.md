This is Imbued with Love. No filters. No photoshop. We’re just fading the light in the room.  
It is 110x90cm, created from optical fibre textiles, woven specifically for this piece. The pattern in the fabric comes from a knitted dress made by my mother for my daughter, each stitch an expression of generational love.   
The patterns of light were made by my beloved husband, and it uses words of love stored on the blockchain to generate its colors. As the digital tokens connected to Love change hands, each new caretaker will get to express their interpretations of Love and change the appearance of the artwork.  
When you look at Imbued with Love, what you see is the expressions of Love from hundreds, or thousands of people. Some strangers, some family. All there, in the moment.  
We’re almost ready for the exhibition, which will open on December 10 at Hotel El Ganzo, San Jose del Cabo, Mexico. It will then be put on auction on December 27, live streamed through our webpage.

What does it mean to buy one of these?  
Imbue your NFT  
As the caretaker of an NFT Weave, you are invited to a ritual where you put their interpretation of art into words. These are stored permanently on the blockchain and are used to create the color and light that flows through the physical artwork itself, somewhere in the world.  
Show off your art  
As a Weave caretaker, you will get access to high resolution photo and video material of the artwork that is unique to your NFT. These are made for you to display on a digital photo frame or screen in your home. You could consider this to be similar to owning a signed limited edition copy of an artwork. It's not the same as owning the real thing, but it's also not the same as buying a mass produced poster.   
It's just the beginning  
An NFT forms permanent relationship between the artist and her community. It's an opportunity for patrons and fans to invest in art they love and be able to get something back. As the project goes on, we will provide additional perks and opportunities for our Weave caretakers. A few surprises can be expected.
