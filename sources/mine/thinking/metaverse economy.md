Basics of in-game economy design notes:  
* Consumable assets
  
    * Currency
  
    * Energy
  
    * Resources
  
![Non](Attachments/large-3.png)  
  
TIME IS THE MOST SACRED GAME RESOURCE  
Knowing that, you need to compare all other game values to time. Maybe those game values are challenges, levels, and rewards; in all cases, you need to determine how long it should take the player to beat, conquer, or earn them.   
  
Gamer types: Explorers, Achievers, Explorers, Killers  
  
Resources:  
* Investment Resources (makes you progress faster - gold, items)
  
* Non-Investment Resources (does not affect progress - outfits)
  
* Progress Index (how far have you progressed - experience points)
