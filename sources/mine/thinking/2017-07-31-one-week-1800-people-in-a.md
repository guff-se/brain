One week, 1800 people in a city we built ourselves. I say "city" because this was the year it really became that. A myriad of backstreets, plazas, houses and towers. A place to get lost and found, way too much to see and too much going on to even bother trying. A warm embrace that lifts you up to your highest heights, ruthlessly pushes your limits, but catches with warmth and care in your moment of weakness.
Thank you, to all the souls who invested passion and sweat to create this. Thank you, universe, for allowing such a wondrous entity to emerge.
I am completely taken away, and I am so fucking proud to be a part of this and to call The Borderland my Home.
