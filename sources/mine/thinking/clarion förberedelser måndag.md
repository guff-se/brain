**1. IDOART**

- **I**ntention – What is the intention of the process, and why?

- **Utfall** – What specific outcomes should be achieved by the end of the workshop?

- **A**genda – How will the workshop achieve the desired outcome? What activities will you go through during the day to bring you closer to the desired outcome?

- **Roller** – What roles or responsibilities need to be in place for the workshop to run smoothly? Who is facilitating, and who is participating? Who is documenting, and who is keeping track of the time? What do you expect of the participants?

- **Regler** – What guidelines will be in place during the day? What can you expect from each other? Is it ok to have your cell phone on? To criticise inputs during brainstorming? Let the participants add rules to ensure that they have ownership of them.

- **Tid** – What is the expected time for the workshop, including breaks, food, and at what time will the day end.
