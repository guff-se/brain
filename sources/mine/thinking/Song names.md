1. Almost purely Piano based song. Chopin inspired. Brittle low fi piano sound. A lot of reverb and reverse reverb. Emotional journey: sadness, brittleness, vulnerability, it’s ok.  
2. The sound of rain. A recording of 3 small kids playing in the park, far away from the microphone. Initially dark and mechanical sounds, then disrupted by a glockenspiel playing a sad waltz in melodic minor. Finally a major chord to bring it home. Emotional journey: childhood, long lost, innocence, sadness, forgiveness.  
3. A flute that is mechanically broken and distorted. Then rhythmic chords, resolving in a warm and homey clarinets. Emotional journey: spaciousness, sadness, caretaking, home, being close to your mother.  
4. Main feature is a bass synth with along glissando from low to high note, back and fourth along with the breath. Ending with a harp plucking chord arpeggios. Emotional journey: Sharpness, something challenging yet sweet and rewarding, tension and release, beauty, a temple. Healing through beauty.  
5. Singing bowls, gongs, metal pipes. Soft synth sounds. Ambient soundscape, low dynamics, slow. Emotional journey: solitary, a walk, a journey, through the woods, something beautiful opening up, monastery on top of a mountain. You have everything you need within you.  
6. Main feature is a shepherds tone, moving infinitely upwards. Resolves in a soft clarinet. Emotional journey: stress, tension, discomfort, then an extreme release when this stress is released, the feeling of coming home after a really intense and rough experience.  
7. Crickets, spacey synth sounds, the sound of thunder. Emotional journey: close to nature, a beautiful view, solitary, healing, Mother Earth.  
8. Chimes, soft pads, the persistent sound of a heartbeat through the entire song, synth pulses and rhythms. Slow and soft. Emotional journey: feeling your body, an embodied experience, being in touch with yourself, your breath, your heartbeat.  
9. Metal string sounds, soft synth bells. Then suddenly the sound of trains, the subway of Paris, a voice calling out the next train in the PA, people walking past, trains passing. Strings. Bells. Emotional journey: travel, starting at home, moving though the chaos of civilization, humans, social intensity, then arriving to your destination.  
10. Strings and clarinet. Very acoustic sounding. The clarinet carries the song, breathy, heavy and crisp. Emotional journey: childhood, the home you grew up in. Walking through memories of the past.    
11. A guitar with 1/4 delay, picking a slow melody throughout the song. A bit of a psychedelic rock feeling. A rhythmic bell opening up in the treble. Then a rhythmic bass, pounding, pushing, with the soft guitar on top. Emotional journey: Friendship, in company with people.  
12. A sine wave, one half note off in each ear, creating a kind of binaural experience. A shaker, rainmaker. The shaker accelerates and explodes in a shower of delay and reverb. Silence. A church bell starts striking, then a full church organ playing an epic psalm. Emotional journey: discomfort, feeling exposed, facing adversity. Then being saved, turning to God, the sense of awe.
