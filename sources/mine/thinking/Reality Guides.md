Process to go through when someone gets a role:

- Role assigned
- Check in with experts on the role
- What tasks and responsibilities does the role entails?
- What is the larger context of the role? What other roles will you be interacting with?
- Try to come up with any tasks or responsibilities that might be missing in the role.
- Should you do all this, or should the role be adjusted or split?
- Looking around, is there any role missing in your area? If so, work with reality guide to establish that role.
- Reiterate 3-6.
