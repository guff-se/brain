A year and a half ago, I met with @[6527658217:274:Dennis Kucinich] for the first time. We had a long night of lovely brain sparring, talking politics, philosophy and Burning Man. We were also speculating about how a future presidential run for Dennis would look.
One of the central themes of the conversation was how powerless a president actually is, and how a future campaign would have to do something different than just elect another figurehead. We talked about a campaign that actually had the guts to express the futility of presidential power alone, instead of promising "change" that is impossible to deliver.
I was watching this clip with @[100044424909727:2048:Bernie Sanders] today, echoing the exact ideas, in the exact language that we were using. It really got me feeling that we were on to something, that we ARE on to something.
Over the last year, I have often fallen in to cynicism and hopelessness when looking at the climate of the current political debate. Today I see a sliver of light... Maybe it is possible to fix democracy?
