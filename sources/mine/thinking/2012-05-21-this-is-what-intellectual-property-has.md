This is what intellectual property has come to: A consortium of rich companies systematically suing the shit out of anyone that comes close to touching patents that were not invented by them and are not used by them.
IP has nothing to do with innovation, nothing to do with entrepreneurship. It's a institutionalized system for big corporations to bully everyone smaller than themselves out of business.
