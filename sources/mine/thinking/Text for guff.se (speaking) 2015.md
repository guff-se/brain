Gustaf Josefsson

I’m a speaker, entrepreneur, artist and philosopher. 

The Future:

-> flux

No matter if you look at the history of the earth,

the history of humanity or the history of technology,

the same pattern emerges: It all seem to happen at the end.

Why is change changing faster?

The Future is unpredictable.

-> co-creation

There are 2.6 billion smart phone users today.

There will be 6 billion by the end of the decade.

From YouTube to Burning Man

Capitalism and democracy.

Culture and technology, markets and media.

The Future is created by us all.

-> meaning

one

The Future is non-transactional
