A Your faith was strong but you needed proof

A You saw her bathing on the roof

B Her beauty in the moonlight overthrew you

C She tied you to a kitchen chair

C She broke your throne, and she cut your hair

B And from your lips she drew the Hallelujah

We put our feet into the sand

From water we came on to land

Through our flesh the spirit rips

and in passion from our fumbling lips

came nothing but a nonsense hallelujah

I looked at you and you looked at me

we spoke the words that made us free

the 

we wept because of freedom lost

and gathered round our empty cross

and out to nothing we cried hallelujah

we sing in the holy nonsense hallelujah
