Questions to ask yourself?

- Is the expense _needed_ for the development of The Borderland?
- Can the problem be solved in another way?
- What can you do to make the expense less expensive? 
- If the expense is regarding travel, can someone else that lives closer do it instead?
- Can this expense be covered through a dream grant instead? The dreams platform is the primary budgeting platform for community projects. Any expense that can be covered through that system should be.
- Can the Borderland's current financial situation fully support the expense? Seek advice on the expense from someone with good insight into the budget.
- Is the expenditure trend sound? ie if a lot of expenses has been made recently, that might be a good reason to stop and check the sanity of the expenses.
Who should you ask for advice?

- The larger the expense, the more people you should ask for advice.
- Always ask someone who have an overview of the current financial situation. This is probably someone on the board.
- Find out if anyone have gotten reimbursed for a similar expense before and ask for their advice.
- Find experts on the type of effort you are trying to make (infrastructure, power, government relations), and ask them for their advice.

Providing you go through this list carefully, the decision is yours to make. Reimbursements are then done by filling out this form to get your money paid out. [link]
