received through your own NWTA.  
What’s at Risk?  
There is an obvious risk involved in arranging an NWTA in the midst of the ongoing Covid-19 situation. The Council of MKP Nordic has given careful consideration to the health recommendations of the Swedish government, and given Sweden’s emphasis on individual choice and responsibility in response to these recommendations, a decision has been made to go ahead with an NWTA with a maximum 50 men in total. There is at the moment a temporary ban on gatherings larger than 50 people. So, we will accept 20 participants and 30 staff to include leaders and one kitchen king (no MOS).  
   
If you apply to staff, you expressly accept that:  
   
• a zero-risk environment during the weekend in terms of virus transmission cannot be guaranteed.  
• whereas certain provisions will be made, this weekend will entail shared areas and proximity, and no masks.  
• you will step down and give up your place to another man if you come into contact with a known Covid-19 infected person within 14 days of arrival on site  
• if you are not in good health, or have symptoms, even mild symptoms, of Covid-19 on the day before arrival you shall not participate but let me know asap & give up your place to another man.  
• if you belong to a Covid-19 risk group or take care of people in these groups you should not be signing up.  
• this NWTA may be cancelled at short notice due to circumstances beyond our control, such as a new lockdown which prevents travel (in which case your staff fee will be refunded).  
• you may need to quarantine on your return from the weekend, depending on your government’s travel stipulations with reference to Sweden.
