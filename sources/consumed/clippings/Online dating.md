Thomas says that people often underestimate the huge cultural shift that online dating has had on society. Research suggests that online dating has led to more [interracial marriages](https://www.economist.com/briefing/2018/08/18/how-the-internet-has-changed-dating), more couples with different religions and levels of education, and also pairings with partners who tend to be [closer in age](http://www.unm.edu/~reubenjthomas/onlineexogamy.pdf) (pdf).
