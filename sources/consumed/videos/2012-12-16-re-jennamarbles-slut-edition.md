In this video, I respond to Jenna Marbles' latest vlog: "Things I Don't Understand About Girls 2: Slut Edition".  I begin by acknowledging that the video embodies a few major myths about sluts and continue on to deconstruct these myths.  Section 1 covers what slut shaming actually is and what kind of mentality it breeds.  Section 2 covers how slut shame comes from the expectation that women earn our approval by obeying social demands of their sexual behavior.  Section 3 covers how slut shame is a way that women "pay for sleeping around" - a sexist double standard that does not affect men.  Section 4 covers the topic of "self respect" and the society-wide need to respect peoples' choices as the first step toward healthy sexuality.
