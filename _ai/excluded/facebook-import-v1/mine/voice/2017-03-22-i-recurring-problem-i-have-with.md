I recurring problem I have with being an "inspiring person" is that people put me on a pedestal and believe that I am smarter and more emotionally balanced person than I actually am. Then they get disappointed with me when I don't live up to their idolized version of me, a version of myself that I never actually claimed to be.
Thank you to all of you who still stick around after you have gotten to know me. It's nice to know that I am more than a teen romance.
