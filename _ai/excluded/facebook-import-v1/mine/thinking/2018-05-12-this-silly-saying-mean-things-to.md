This silly “saying mean things to plants will
make them die”-experiment is making its rounds again. This time IKEA is involving an entire school to try this “scientific experiment”, with the additional motive to teach the lesson that bullying is bad (and junk science is good).
Seriously? I mean, what do you think IKEA would do if it turned out that they didn’t get the result they wanted? In front of hundreds of kids and cameras? It not like failure is an option.
So, I’m not saying they cheated and killed the plant, just pointing out that they have every incentive in the world to cheat and kill the plant.
So the real lesson is: Don’t confuse your pseudoscientific experiment with teaching kids about bullying.
