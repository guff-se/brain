I posted a link saying ”Vitamin D & Omega-3 have a larger effect on depression than antidepressants.”
It turned out that while there is truth to the value of Vitamin D & Omega-3, the effect sizes in the article i linked were a bit cherry picked and exaggerated. When looking at larger metastudies these supplements do not have larger effect on depression than antidepressants. You should still take them, though.

I stand corrected. Instead I would like this post to serve as an inspiration of how to be wrong.
